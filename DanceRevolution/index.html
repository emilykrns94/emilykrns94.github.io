About Us

Young Fresh and Funky, Dance Revolution aims to encourage an active and healthy lifestyle to children, teenagers and adults.Our aim is to provide a safe, structured and fun environment for everyone to take part in classes, it is a great way for children to mix & meet new friends. We take part in lots of exciting events throughout the year, we have introduced fitness into children's lives through dance and co-ordination through interactive games and dance to create a fun environment, while learning a lot of fun dance routines, Our instructors are totally focused all the students while making everyone welcome.
