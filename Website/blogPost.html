Fethard Girls Do Well At Hip-Hop

Well done to Fethard girls who won trophies at the All-Ireland Hip Hop dancing championships, which were held last Sunday at the City North Hotel, Gormanstown, Co. Meath. The three girls, Ciara Connolly, Katie O'Flynn and Amy Brophy, are all members of Dance Revolution who promote an active and healthy lifestyle amongst children, teenagers and adults through dance, a friendly atmosphere where you meet new friends, have fun and stay fit at the same time. Dance Revolution also take part in lots of exciting community street events.

Dance Revolution Dance Studios qualify for European and World Competitions

>Dance Revolution Dance Studios based across Tipperary, recently qualified for the Europeans in Germany 2015 and the worlds in Glasgow this August 2015.5 crews “adrenaline” placing 1st, “acceler8″ 2nd place, “explosive” 1st place, “rage” 1st place and “insanity” placing 3rd.The school brought home 72 medals and 49 trophies while all qualifying for the Worlds and Europeans.We are so proud of everyone and look forward to a great year ahead with Dance Revolution, we cater from ages 3+ and all welcome to join competitions optional.
