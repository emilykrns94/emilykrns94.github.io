About Us

Dance Revolution was founded in 2010 by Rebecca Neill with the hope of changing the lives of her students. Dance Revolution aims to encourage an active and healthy lifestyle to children, teenagers and adults. Their aim is to provide a safe, structured and fun environment for everyone to take part in classes, it is a great way for children to mix and meet new friends. They have introduced fitness and co-ordination into children's lives through dance and also through interactive games. Dance Revolution focuses 100% on all of their dancers building confidence while making friends and working out in a fun and friendly atmosphere. They also hold also many activities throughout the year such as street events, dance camps, shows and workshops to encourage their students.

Meet The Team

Rebecca Neill

Rebecca Neill is a 27 year old fully qualified fitness instructor and has been dancing since she was a child. She began teaching 13 years ago and created Dance Revolution in 2010 with the hope of changing the lives of her students. Her dancers have increased their confidence since joining Dance Revolution. She has been working with the public for many years.

Lauryn Boyle

Lauryn Boyle is a member of the team and has been dancing since the age of 5. She has been a member of Dance Revolution since the beginning and since joining Dance Revolution she has become an all-Ireland hip hop champion placing first in 2012. She has appeared on RTE’s talent show “Show off or get off” in 2013. She loves to teach at Dance Revolution as she has the opportunity to pass her knowledge and experience on to the younger dancers.

Dean Kenny

Dean Kenny is a 15 year old and one of Dance Revolutions choreographer. Dean has been dancing since he was 8. He loves to dance mainly break dancing but has a love for all styles of hip-hop and dance in general. He recently started choreographing for Dance Revolution in the hopes of passing his experience onto other dancers. He hopes to continue dancing and one day work in the dance industry.
